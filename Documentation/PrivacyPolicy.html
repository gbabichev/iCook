Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this app (“the App”) handles your information.

Core Principles

  • iCloud Sync: All recipes and collections you create are securely synced to your iCloud account. This keeps your recipes up‑to‑date across your Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac).
  • Encryption: Your recipes are encrypted in transit and at rest through iCloud's security infrastructure.
  • No collection, no tracking: We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data beyond iCloud sync. We do not track usage, collect analytics, or access your content directly.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect analytics, usage metrics, crash reports, or identifiers.
  • We do not use third‑party SDKs, trackers, or ad networks.
  • We do not store data on our own external servers or with third-party services (only with Apple's iCloud, which you control).
  • We do not have access to your recipes — they are directly synced from your device to your iCloud account.

Your Choices

iCloud Sync: All recipes you create are synced to your iCloud account to keep them up‑to‑date across your Apple devices.

Sharing: If you choose to share a collection, iCloud Sharing is used to invite collaborators. You control who has access and can stop sharing at any time.

Export: You can export your data into a readable JSON file using the Mac version of the app.

Any time you export, copy, or share content using your operating system’s share or file features, that action is under your control.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date above and include the revised policy in the app repository or website.
