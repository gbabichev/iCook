‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new recipe in iCook?
Tap the "+" button to add a recipe. You can enter the recipe name, time, details, steps, and add a photo.
How do I organize recipes with categories?
Create categories to group recipes (Breakfast, Dinner, Desserts, etc.). Use the Categories list in the sidebar to add, rename, or remove categories.
How do I add steps and ingredients?
Open a recipe, then add steps under Recipe Steps. Each step can include its own ingredients. You can reorder steps or collapse/expand them as you edit.
How do I share a collection?
Open Collections, tap Share on a collection, and invite people. You can manage or stop sharing at any time.
Is iCook free to use?
Yes. iCook is free with no ads or tracking.
Will my recipes sync across my devices?
Yes. iCook uses iCloud to keep your recipes and collections in sync across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

üîß Troubleshooting

iCloud Sync Issues

If your recipes aren't syncing across devices:

  • Ensure you're signed in to the same iCloud account on all devices
  • Check that iCloud Drive is enabled in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud
  • Make sure you have enough iCloud storage available
  • Restart the app or your device if sync seems stuck

Recipes Not Appearing

If you can't find your recipes:

  • Check if the recipe is in a different category or collection
  • Use search to locate a recipe by name
  • Make sure you‚Äôre viewing the right collection

Performance or Freezing

If iCook seems slow or unresponsive:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Restart your device
  • Update iCook to the latest version from the App Store
